Fettuccine Alfredo

This rich and creamy fettuccine Alfredo recipe is easy enough for beginner cooks, but it’s impressive enough for company.

These are the simple ingredients you’ll need to make this fettuccine Alfredo recipe. Pasta, of course you'll need fettuccine pasta. Butter, this Alfredo sauce starts with two sticks of butter. Cream, The rich sauce calls for almost a cup of heavy cream. Seasonings, The fettuccine is simply seasoned with salt, pepper, and garlic salt. Last but not least, the cheese. Not just any kind of cheese, specifically Romano and Parmesan cheese.

Ingredients

  • 24 ounces dry fettuccine pasta
  • 1 cup of butter
  • 1 dash garlic salt
  • ¾ pint heavy cream
  • ¾ cup grated Romano cheese
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • salt and pepper to taste
